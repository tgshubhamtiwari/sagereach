Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Welcome to SageReach. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit https://www.thesagereach.com/ or use our services.

Our services include:

  • Social Media Management
  • Social Media Ads
  • Graphic Design
  • Logo Design
  • Video Editing
  • Website

By using our website or our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services.

a. Personal Information

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Company or brand name
  • Payment or billing details (if needed)

b. Non-Personal Information

  • Your browser and device type
  • IP address and location (city or country)
  • Pages you visit and time spent on the site

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and manage our services
  • Talk with you about projects or support
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Send updates or offers (only if you agree)
  • Follow legal rules if needed

3. Sharing Your Information

We never sell your information. We may share it only with:

  • Trusted partners who help us run our business (for example, payment or hosting companies)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Keeping Your Data Safe

We take security seriously and use safe methods to protect your data. Still, please understand that no system on the internet is completely safe.

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to make your experience better. Cookies help us understand how visitors use our site. You can turn off cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the site may not work properly if you do.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • See what personal data we have about you
  • Ask us to fix or delete your data
  • Ask us to stop sending marketing emails

You can contact us anytime at hello@thesagereach.com.

7. Data Storage

We keep your data only as long as we need it for business or legal reasons. After that, we delete it safely.

8. Links to Other Websites

Our website may have links to other websites, such as social media platforms. We are not responsible for how those websites handle your information. Please read their privacy policies before sharing any data.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not for children under 13. We do not collect data from children knowingly. If we find that a child has shared data with us, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date on this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

  • Email: hello@thesagereach.com
  • Website: https://www.thesagereach.com/
